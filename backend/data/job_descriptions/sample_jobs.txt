# Sample Job Descriptions for Testing

## Senior Software Engineer - Microservices
We are looking for a Senior Software Engineer to join our team. The ideal candidate will have experience with:
- Building scalable backend systems and microservices
- Implementing automated testing and CI/CD pipelines  
- Working with REST APIs and database optimization
- Leading technical teams and mentoring junior developers
- Experience with machine learning and data processing
- Strong problem-solving skills and ability to work in fast-paced environments

The role involves architecting high-performance systems, implementing best practices for code quality, and collaborating with cross-functional teams to deliver innovative solutions.

## Data Scientist - AI/ML
We're seeking a Data Scientist with expertise in machine learning and artificial intelligence. Key requirements include:
- Advanced degree in Computer Science, Statistics, or related field
- Experience with Python, R, and SQL
- Knowledge of machine learning frameworks (TensorFlow, PyTorch, scikit-learn)
- Experience with data visualization and statistical analysis
- Strong communication skills for presenting findings to stakeholders
- Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, Azure)

## Product Manager - AI Products
Looking for a Product Manager to lead our AI product initiatives. The ideal candidate will have:
- 5+ years of product management experience
- Experience with AI/ML products and technologies
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
- Experience with user research and data-driven decision making
- Excellent communication and leadership skills
- Technical background preferred

## DevOps Engineer - Cloud Infrastructure
We need a DevOps Engineer to manage our cloud infrastructure. Requirements include:
- Experience with AWS, Azure, or GCP
- Knowledge of containerization (Docker, Kubernetes)
- Experience with infrastructure as code (Terraform, CloudFormation)
- Strong scripting skills (Python, Bash, PowerShell)
- Experience with CI/CD pipelines and automation
- Knowledge of monitoring and logging tools
